Learning the Game

From your first swing to your first match — the fundamentals that form the foundation of every great tennis player.

Overview

Learning tennis properly from the start makes everything easier later. Good technique learned early becomes instinct; bad habits learned early require years of correction. This section covers the technical foundations every developing player needs to understand — from grip and stance to swing mechanics and court positioning.

Whether you're brand new to the game or looking to rebuild your technical base, these fundamentals are where championship tennis begins.
