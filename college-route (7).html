Understanding the Landscape

The NCAA world is complex. We simplify it.

Division I, II, III — NAIA — Junior College. Each path has different rules, timelines, and opportunities. We help you understand which is right for you.

Dive Deeper →
NCAA Division I

The highest level. Scholarship opportunities available. Early recruiting timelines and strict contact rules require expert navigation.

NCAA Division II

Competitive athletics with strong scholarship opportunities. Often an undervalued path with excellent academic and athletic balance.

NCAA Division III

No athletic scholarships, but strong academic financial aid. Known for high academic quality and genuine student-athlete balance.

NAIA & Junior College

Alternative pathways with scholarship opportunities and flexible recruiting timelines — often overlooked but highly valuable.
